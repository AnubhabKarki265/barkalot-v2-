Château Barkalot, Est. MMXXIV
Est. MMXXIV

Hark, Noble Visitor!

Château Barkalot was born from a simple quest: to build a cozy gathering place where dogs and their devoted humans could sip extraordinary brews, munch paw-worthy pastries, and forge bonds as legendary as the knights of old.

"Every great realm needs a gathering place. Ours just happens to serve very excellent coffee — and the finest pack of pups in the land."

We opened our drawbridge in the heart of Camelot City, welcoming dogs of all breeds, shapes, and energy levels. Our Round Table seats 18 (one for each noble knight), and the wag-worthy atmosphere is open to all who seek warmth, community, and a truly noble cup.

The Question Scroll

Queries for the Herald

Hark! Each Round Table seat accommodates one human and up to two dogs. Our tables seat 4–6, so a party of four may bring up to 8 noble pups. Larger parties: contact our heralds in advance.

Yes — the designated play arena is fully off-leash. The main café floor requires leashes. All pups must be on-leash upon entry and exit through our castle gatehouse.

Walk-ins are always welcome! However, during jousting nights and special events the realm fills quickly. We recommend a reservation on weekends. Use the contact form below or call the heralds.

Every pup is knighted upon entry, regardless of breed or size. We do ask that dogs with a history of aggression be introduced gradually and muzzled in busy communal spaces — for the safety of all knights.

Core vaccines (rabies, DHPP, Bordetella) must be current. We may ask to see your pup's health passport on first visit. Proof of vaccination is required for play area access.

Verily, we love all noble creatures — but for the peace of the realm, Château Barkalot is a dogs-only establishment. We are working on a sister café, "Château Meowlot," for our feline allies.

All adoptable pups are partnered with certified rescue organizations. Fill out an interest form, meet your match at our monthly Adoption Fair, and if the paws align, our heralds will guide you through the process.

Gather the pack for birthdays, celebrations, or corporate retreats! We offer full buy-out evenings and partial reservation packages. Contact our heralds with your quest details.
